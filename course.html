Swimming Pool:

  • Dimensions: 50mX15m
  • Depth: 5ft at the shallow end, 18ft at the deep end
  • No. of lanes: 7
  • 7 diving boards at both sides of the pool
  • Equipments: Kickboards, Pull buoy, Hand paddles, finger paddles, flippers
  • Pool water treatment: Pool water is tested in labs every month and treated in the filtration plan
  • Diving platform at heights of 5m and 10m

Lawn Tennis:

Tennis is one of the most popular sports worldwide It is open to people of all ages and can be played by
anyone who can hold the racquet even those in a wheelchair. Tennis is played by millions of people around
the world and has gained prominence as a spectator sport. There are five international standard synthetic surface courts in GNE Ludhiana.

The Timings are:
Morning : 6 AM TO 9 AM
Evening : 4 PM TO 8:30 PM

FootBall:

Football is the world’s most popular ball game in numbers of participants and spectators. Simple in its principal rules and essential equipment, the sport can be played almost anywhere, from official football playing fields.

The Timings are:
Morning: 6 A.M. 8 A.M.
Evening: 4.30 P.M. 8.30 P.M.

Cricket:

Cricket is undoubtedly one of the most loved sports of the country and the GNE Ludhiana Cricket culture was built to nurture this passion of the cricket lovers in the institute. With good pitches, lush green outfield and Nets, GNE offers one of the finest facilities in the country. Our team aims to create a friendly environment, emphasizing the health and fitness of the students, along with preparing them for competitions.

Volleyball:

Volleyball is one of the most interactive games going. It is a game of intuition, imagination, improvisation – but most of all, of reciprocity – of teamwork. Our team aims at the general health and fitness of the students, along with preparing them for competitions. The students are put through efficient training routines under the guidance of experienced coaches. We offer a welcoming, encouraging environment where students can fully develop their skills. This is the place to be if you are passionate about sports. So come, Be a part of this sporting action so that moments last forever!

The timings:
Morning: 6:30 am to 9 am
Evening: 4 pm to 8:30 pm
